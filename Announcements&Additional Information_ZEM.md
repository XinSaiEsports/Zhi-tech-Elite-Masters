# ZHI-TECH ELITE MASTERS

-   Open Registration Period: December 5, 2025, 00:00 to December 12, 2025, 24:00 (Beijing Time)

-   Closed Qualifier Dates: January 17–19, 2026 (Beijing Time)

-   Playoffs Dates: January 23–25, 2026 (Beijing Time)

-   Tournament Operator:XinSai Esports（XSE）。

-   Tournament Tier:Tier 2。

## Team Registration

-   This tournament does not include Direct VRS Invites based on VRS list; all participants will be invited through open registration on a "first-come-first-served" basis.

-   This tournament is open to teams from across the globe.

-   The registration fee per team:3000 RMB(payment via Alipay QR code)

     -   Teams will participate in the Closed Qualifier online at their own expense; the organizer will not reimburse any related costs.

     -    The organizer will cover flight/train transportation to Beijing for teams advancing to the Playoffs.

     -    The organizer will provide accommodation and catering in Beijing for teams advancing to the Playoffs.

     -    The organizer will provide training rooms in Beijing for teams advancing to the Playoffs.

     -    Re-fund policy: If your team needs to withdraw from the tournament before December 15th(Beijing Time) we will try to replace your team with teams on the waiting list. If we find a replacement we will re-fund the full amount. If teams withdraw or fail to lock in lineup after December 15th(Beijing Time) no refund will be made.

-   A total of 16 teams will be accepted through open registration. Registration is only valid once the payment has been completed via the Alipay QR code sent by email. Limited slots available!

-   Initial seeding will be determined according to the VRS list as of January 2026.

     -    4.Pay for the registration fee within 24h to lock in/secure your spot


## Closed Qualifier

-   This stage will be held online.

-   The game server will be an online server located in Beijing, China.

-   Format: Single-elimination.

    -    All matches are Bo3.   

## Playoffs

-   This stage will be held offline.

-   Format: Single-elimination bracket.

    -    The final is Bo5, and all other matches are Bo3
